About Our Hotel

Why The Madras Grand?

Aptly named after the yesteryear name for Namma Chennai, The Madras Grand is a conflux of convenience and old-world charm in one place. Situated in a central location in the Northern part of Chennai, The Madras Grand is the perfect little place to bring your family for a quick getaway or find a place to relax during your business trips. The Madras Grand combines local flavours while being affordably luxurious.
