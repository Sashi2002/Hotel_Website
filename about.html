About Madras Grand

OUR GOAL IS TO PROVIDE QUALITY HOSPITALITY SERVICE AT AFFORDABLE PRICES.

Madras Grand, Chennai, located in the heart of the city, is just a short distance away from the TIDEL Park which houses Cognizant, Tata Consultancy Services and HCL Technologies. Opposite Raj Bhavan, it is also easily accessible from Anna University and Indian Institute of Technology, Chennai.
